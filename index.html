I was born in Tianjin, China in 1993 and has been living there before I was 18. In 2011, I got admitted into Hohai University and started my career in water engineering ever since. After completing my B.E. in Agricultural Water Conservancy Engineering in 2015, I went to UC Irvine to continue my study in Hydrology & Water Resources as a master student. I spent two wonderful years there and happily graguated. After that I went back to my home country in 2017 and had been working at the CRAES in Beijing for a while.
In 2019, I came back to the U.S to pursue my PhD under the supervision of Dr. Tiantian Yang at the University of Oklahoma.
I am particularly interested in computational modeling and optimization theory.
