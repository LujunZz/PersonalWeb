Brief Bio

I was born in Tianjin, China in 1993 and has been living there until I turned 18. In 2011, I was admitted into Hohai University and started my career in water engineering ever since. After completing my B.E. in Agricultural Water Conservancy Engineering in 2015, I went to UC Irvine to continue my study in Hydrology & Water Resources as a master student. I went back to China with my Master's in 2017 and worked at the CRAES in China for a while.
In 2019, I came back to the U.S again and am currently a PhD student at the University of Oklahoma at Norman.
I am particularly interested in computational modeling and optimization theory.
