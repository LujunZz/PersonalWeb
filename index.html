My CV
I was born in Tianjin, China in 1993 and has been living there before I was 18. In 2011, I was admitted into Hohai University hence started my career in water engineering ever since. After completing my B.E. in Agricultural Water Conservancy Engineering in 2015, I went to UC Irvine to continue my study in Hydrology & Water Resources as a master student. I spent a little bit more than two happy years there and graguated. After that I went back to my home country in 2017 and has been working at the CRAES in Beijing, China for a while.
In 2019, I came back to the U.S to pursue my PhD under the supervision of Dr. Tiantian Yang at the University of Oklahoma at Norman.
I am particularly interested in computational modeling and optimization theory.
